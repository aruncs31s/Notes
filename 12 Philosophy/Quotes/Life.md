# Life

The universe will show you the right person,  but in the wrong time, on purpose. Not because to torture you but to show you that its possible, to give you the glims of love that you deserve before you are ready to hold it , you met them too early while you are still healing from someone else, when you are building your own carrier , your own life and yourself , when you are in different cities, different chapter and different version of your self , who you are gonna become , and it hurts because you feel it, they feel it , but life doesn't line up. So at the last you let them go and for years you wonder what if we had met in a   right time?. But here is the very harsh truth that most people miss. Meeting them at the wrong wasn't a mistake actually, it was a preparation. Cause when the time finally align, you will know that what exactly you are holding and you wont let it go.
