# Ethernet

#### Standard Connection

_Front facing the clip on the top , left to right_
1 -> White Orenge
2 -> Orenge
3 -> White Green
4 -> Blue
5 -> White Blue
6 -> Green
7 -> White Brown
8 -> Brown
*this is the normal connection*
- Blue , White Blue -> -ve (PoE)
- Grey , White Grey -> +ve (PoE)

#### Cross Connection 
_Front facing the clip on the top , left to right_
1 -> White Green
2 -> Green
3 -> White Orenge
4 -> Blue
5 -> White Blue
6 -> Orenge
7 -> White Brown
8 -> Brown
