# Shannon Limit

$$
C = B \cdot \log \left( 1 + \frac{S}{N} \right)
$$

C – Channel capacity [bits/sec]

B – Bandwidth of channel [Hz]

S – Average received signal power [watts]

N – Average noise power [watts]
