#### Design

$$
V_{out} = V_{in2} \frac{R_f  + R_i}{R_i} \left( \frac{R_2}{R_1+ R_2} \right) - V_{in1} \frac{R_f}{R_i} \tag{1}
$$
**Special Case**
When $R_{1} = R_{i}$ and $R_{2} =R_{f}$ 
$$
V_{out} = \left(V_{in2}  - V_{in1}\right) \frac{R_{f}}{R_{i}}
$$
