# PCF8574
This ic is used in [[I2C LCD Interfacing|I2C LCD]] , it converts the i2c data to parallel , also called port expander
 
- GND: Pin 6 (GND)
- VCC: Pin 4 (5V)
- SDA: Pin 3 (SDA)
- SCL: Pin 5 (SCL)
- RS: 15
- RW: 18
- E: 16
- Data 4-7: 21, 22, 23, 24
